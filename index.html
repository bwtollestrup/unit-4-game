Game Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four glasses of wine below. By clicking on a glass you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You will win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each glass is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each glass.


Pour yourself a glass of wine and enjoy!
